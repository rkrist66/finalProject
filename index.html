Denmark 2013

My dad was born in Hjorring, Denmark and imigrated to Salt Lake City, Utah when he was 21. My life is full of Danish culture and tradition. It has always been a desire to visit Denmark with my dad before he passed on.

My dad is now 75 years old and my mom has passed away. Dad wanted to take a trip back to the homeland to visit family and friends but did not want to go alone, so he asked me to join him.

We had a great trip and here are a few memories of the trip.

Farm

My dad's family grew up on a farm in a small town named Krustrup. They would grow crops and owned the traditional farm animals. I grew up hearing stories of life on the farm and the farm has become a shrine for our family.

The farm is now owned by my cousin Niels so I had the priviledge to stay for a couple of weeks on the farm (and save a ton of money on hotel costs).
