Denmark 2013

My dad was born in Hjorring, Denmark and came to Salt Lake City on a visit to see the LDS Temple. Well, he met my mom and immediately applied for US Citizenship and never went back... except for an occasional vacation.

My dad is now 75 years old and my mom has passed away. Dad wanted to take a trip back to the homeland to visit family and friends but did not want to go alone, so he asked me to join him.

We had a great trip and here are a few memories of the trip.
